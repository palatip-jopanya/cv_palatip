Palatip Jopanya

Palatip is a Thai national currently pursuing Master's degree in Communication Systems at Linkoping University, which he began in the fall of 2022. Prior to this, He worked as a RAN Engineer at Nokia (Thailand) Co., Ltd. from May 2018 and Novembor 2021, and as an Integration Engineer at Carenet (Recruitment) for Ericsson (Thailand) project from 2015 to 2018 (contracts). He graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Chiang Mai University in Thailand.
