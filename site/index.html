Palatip Jopanya


As a former RAN engineer at Nokia (Thailand), I am delighted that, in November 2021, I opted to voluntarily exit Nokia to attend Linköping University to pursue my master's degree in Communication Systems. This allowed me to realize my goal of developing my career path in R&D or software development in the wireless industry. I have studied and passed all the courses throughout my first academic year. However, given my limited financial resources, I feel like returning to the workforce rather than continuing my education.


I've worked at vendors as a fulltime engineer and sometimes as a contractor for a cumulative period of more than 8 years as a Radio Access Network Engineer and Integration Engineer. I possessed knowledge of reading the protocol stack for control-plane, Layer 3 messages, physical layer, 3GPP standardization, and UNIX/LINUX command line. I have clear knowledge in 5GC SA/NSA protocol and architecture, 4G EPC includes PCRF, MME, SGW, PGW and I also familiar with Nokia, Ericsson, and Huawei's RAN element’s in configuration, protocol stack in RAN, and software for PM, CM, and FM. Programming languages I am familiar with include C++, C, Python, MATLAB, JAVA, PHP, MySQL, and CSS.
